INTRODUCTION

Aira is an on-demand, autonomous, passenger drone service. The Aira app will allow you to book our electronic vertical take off and landing (eVTOL) vehicles, and provide clear multi-modal booking and navigational features to get you to our vertiports and your destination.

FEATURES

Aira was built to meet user needs and address current pain points with transit. Using a holistic and iterative design process, we were able to narrow our design down to the critical features. We've used Principle, Affter Effects, and Framer to demonstrate some of the functionality.
